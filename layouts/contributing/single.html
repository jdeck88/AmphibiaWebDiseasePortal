To contribute data to the Amphibian Disease Portal, upload your data into GEOME. GEOME will perform data validation and hosts all data that is accessible on the Amphibian Disease Portal. Following are the steps for loading data.
  1. Navigate to GEOME
  2. Create an account if you do not already have one.
  3. Create a project and select that you want to be part of a Team.
  4. During project creation, select the "AmphibiaWeb's Disease Portal" Team
  5. Indicate if you want your project to be public or private and then Create your project.
  6. Once your project is created, goto "Project Settings" and fill out the additional project metadata fields
  7. Click on "Generate Template" on the left side of the screen. Choose the fields you want to include in your project and then click on "Export Excel"
  8. Fill out your spreadsheet using the template that you just downloaded.
  9. Once you insert your data into the Excel Sheet you can uplaod your own spreadsheet into GEOME.
  10. When your spreadsheet is uploaded it will be accessible in the Amphibian Disease portal. Note that you will need to make your project public before it appears in the AD portal.
